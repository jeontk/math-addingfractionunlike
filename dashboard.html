This page suggests learning activities for mastering new terms, concepts, and procedures.

  • Flashcards: Practice key terms and definitions using spaced repetition.
  • Self-quizzes: Test recall of important facts.
  • Compare & Contrast: Organize examples to identify similarities and differences.
  • Categorization tasks: Group related ideas to build a conceptual framework.
  • Procedure builder: Arrange rules step by step until you can perform them from memory.
